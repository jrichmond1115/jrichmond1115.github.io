About Page

Joseph Richmond

My name is Joseph Richmond and I am a 17 year old artist from the Cleveland area. I work on projects for my school, St. Ignatius High School, for myself, and for clients.

I like to experiement with a wide variety of mediums before settling on one to focus toward. I've tried out various types of drawing, painting, and sculputre in my drawing classes, AP Art, and on my own. My portfolio this year was mostly revolved around the idea of "resilience" and I'll explain that in more depth in the collecetion on this site. I definitely took a dive into expressive portraiture painting and hope to explore something different next year.

I have done several commissions before, anywhere from volunteer pieces for departments at school to paid 250 square foot murals and series of acrylic paintings. If you are interested in some of my artwork, custom or existing, send me a message on the Contact page and I'll get back to you!

Thanks for checking out my page!
