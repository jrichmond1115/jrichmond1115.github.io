Mural

Joseph Richmond

Welcome to my Mural page!

This project was by far the coolest part of my art journey. After a series luck, falling into the hands of the right people in the the right place, I was able to recieve this commission to paint a Harley mural on the 250 sq. ft. wall of one of my school administrator's basement walls. The process went from concepts, to design, to finished product. She's decking out the rest of the basement as a surprise "man cave" for her husband.

Oh and don't worry, she gave me permission to get the carpet nice and dirty, she wanted to replace it long before I got there!
